Target audience

The audience will be young sellers of the city who would like to be part of a network of other sellers, making connections, and deals, to get to improve their business.
One important thing is that the target audience is the online sellers who manage online stores whether by social media or a webpage.

Scenarios

#1

A visitor wants to improve his social media business and to do so he checks the list of members of the chamber, checks if there is some graphic designer or similar, and asks for help. He and the designer contact each other and get to an agreement.

#2

To gain more clients all the members organize a fair to show their work, capture the attention of the people, and get new members.

#3

A new member is buying new technology supplies for his business, as a member of the chamber, he has a discount at one of the member's stores to buy electronic devices.
